# Usage Guidelines by Medium

Colour usage guidelines by medium, grounded in colour psychology and usability best practices. {{ className: 'lead' }}

---

## Digital Applications (Web & Mobile) {{ id: 'digital-applications' }}

<Properties>
  <Property name="Primary Background">
    White (#FFFFFF) or Black (#171717) depending on context
  </Property>
  <Property name="Body Text">
    Black 800 (#262626) on light, Slate 200 (#E2E8F0) on dark
  </Property>
  <Property name="Headings">
    Black 900 (#171717) on light, White or Yellow on dark
  </Property>
  <Property name="CTAs & Buttons">
    Yellow (#FFCB05) with black text for primary actions
  </Property>
  <Property name="Status Indicators">
    Traffic light system (Green/Amber/Red) with icons
  </Property>
</Properties>

### Why These Choices?

- **White/Black backgrounds**: Maximum contrast for extended reading
- **Warm greys for text**: Reduces eye strain vs pure black (#000000)
- **Yellow for CTAs**: High visibility and energy, proven to increase conversions
- **Traffic light system**: Universally understood, works for colour blind users

## Print Materials {{ id: 'print-materials' }}

<Properties>
  <Property name="Primary Colours">
    Yellow (#FFCB05) and Black (#171717)
  </Property>
  <Property name="Paper Stock">
    White or cream - never yellow paper (reduces legibility)
  </Property>
  <Property name="Body Text">
    Black (#171717) - 100% ink for maximum readability
  </Property>
  <Property name="Accents">
    Yellow highlights, underlines, and backgrounds
  </Property>
</Properties>

### Print-Specific Guidelines

- **Yellow backgrounds**: Use for callouts, boxes, and highlights - not full pages
- **Black on yellow**: Ensure yellow is solid (not screened) for adequate contrast
- **Yellow on black**: Creates premium, high-end feel for covers and hero sections
- **Avoid yellow text**: Never use yellow for body text on white paper

## Social Media {{ id: 'social-media' }}

### Instagram & Facebook

<Properties>
  <Property name="Post Backgrounds">
    Black (#171717) for maximum impact in feed
  </Property>
  <Property name="Text">
    White and Yellow for readability on mobile
  </Property>
  <Property name="Minimum Text Size">
    60px - readable when scrolling quickly
  </Property>
  <Property name="Brand Consistency">
    Yellow highlights on every post for recognition
  </Property>
</Properties>

### LinkedIn

<Properties>
  <Property name="Professional Tone">
    More white backgrounds, black text, yellow accents
  </Property>
  <Property name="Headshots & Graphics">
    Yellow borders or backgrounds to stand out
  </Property>
  <Property name="Documents">
    Black on white with yellow highlights for professionalism
  </Property>
</Properties>

### Best Practices

- **Consistent branding**: Use yellow in every post for instant recognition
- **High contrast**: Black backgrounds with white/yellow text perform best
- **Thumb-stopping**: Bold, ALL CAPS headlines in yellow
- **Mobile-first**: Test all posts on mobile before publishing

## Video Content {{ id: 'video-content' }}

<Properties>
  <Property name="Base Background">
    Black or dark slate for maximum contrast and focus
  </Property>
  <Property name="Animated Elements">
    Yellow shapes, lines, or highlights that guide attention
  </Property>
  <Property name="Text Reveals">
    Glow effects (yellow or electric blue) on text animations
  </Property>
  <Property name="Transitions">
    Gradient wipes (black to gold) between scenes
  </Property>
  <Property name="Text Size">
    Large (60px+) with high contrast - readable when scrolling
  </Property>
</Properties>

### Video-Specific Guidelines

- **Lower thirds**: Black background with yellow text
- **Progress bars**: Yellow to indicate playback position
- **Captions**: White text with black background for accessibility
- **Thumbnails**: Yellow borders or backgrounds for click-through rates
- **Intro/outro**: Black background with yellow brand elements

<Note>
  **Pro Tip**: Yellow progress bars or animated underlines that follow narration increase viewer retention by 41% (source: Wistia 2024 study).
</Note>
