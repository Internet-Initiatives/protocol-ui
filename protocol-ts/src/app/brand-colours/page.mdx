## Usage Guidelines {{ id: 'usage-guidelines' }}

### Yellow (#FFCB05)

<Properties>
  <Property name="Primary Use">
    Backgrounds, buttons, accents, and highlights
  </Property>
  <Property name="Never Use For">
    Body text on white or light backgrounds (fails WCAG contrast)
  </Property>
  <Property name="Best Paired With">
    Black (#171717) for text and elements
  </Property>
  <Property name="Brand Psychology">
    Optimism, energy, confidence, and forward momentum
  </Property>
</Properties>

### Black (#171717)

<Properties>
  <Property name="Primary Use">
    Text, headings, UI elements, and backgrounds
  </Property>
  <Property name="Best Paired With">
    Yellow (#FFCB05) for accents, white for backgrounds
  </Property>
  <Property name="Brand Psychology">
    Premium quality, sophistication, power, and professionalism
  </Property>
  <Property name="Accessibility">
    Excellent contrast on white (21:1 ratio) - exceeds WCAG AAA
  </Property>
</Properties>
