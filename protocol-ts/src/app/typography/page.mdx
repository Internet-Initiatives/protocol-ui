## Best Practices {{ id: 'best-practices' }}

### Body Text

<Properties>
  <Property name="Minimum Size">
    16px (1rem) - Never go below 16px for body text
  </Property>
  <Property name="Line Height">
    1.6 for body text (25.6px at 16px)
  </Property>
  <Property name="Font Weight">
    400 (Regular) for optimal readability
  </Property>
  <Property name="Colour">
    Black 800 (#262626) on light backgrounds, Slate 200 (#E2E8F0) on dark
  </Property>
</Properties>

### Headings

<Properties>
  <Property name="Line Height">
    1.2 for headings - tighter for impact
  </Property>
  <Property name="Font Weights">
    700-900 for maximum presence
  </Property>
  <Property name="Letter Spacing">
    -0.02em for large headings (48px+) to improve optical balance
  </Property>
</Properties>

### Accessibility

- **Minimum contrast**: 4.5:1 for body text (WCAG AA)
- **Large text contrast**: 3:1 for 24px+ or 19px+ bold (WCAG AA)
- **Line length**: 50-75 characters for optimal reading
- **Paragraph spacing**: 1.5em between paragraphs
