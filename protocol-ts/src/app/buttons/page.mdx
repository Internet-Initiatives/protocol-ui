## Accessibility Guidelines

<Properties>
  <Property name="Minimum Size">
    44x44px touch target (WCAG 2.1 Level AAA)
  </Property>
  <Property name="Focus Indicator">
    2px ring with 2px offset, visible in all states
  </Property>
  <Property name="Contrast Ratio">
    4.5:1 minimum for text (all buttons meet this)
  </Property>
  <Property name="Keyboard Accessible">
    Tab to focus, Enter/Space to activate
  </Property>
</Properties>
