## Primary Brand Colour - Yellow {{ id: 'primary-brand-colour' }}

Yellow (#FFCB05) is ONLY used for backgrounds, buttons, and accents. **NEVER for body text** due to poor contrast on white backgrounds.

<Note>
  Yellow is our signature brand colour, but it must be used strategically. The light nature of yellow means it fails WCAG contrast requirements when used as text on white backgrounds. Always use yellow as a background or accent element, paired with dark text.
</Note>
