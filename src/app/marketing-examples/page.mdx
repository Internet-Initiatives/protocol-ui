- **Clear CTA**: Yellow button stands out against black background
- **Brand consistency**: Signature UPA look and feel

## White Backgrounds (Yellow as Accent Only) {{ id: 'white-backgrounds' }}

On white backgrounds, yellow is used sparingly as an accent. Black dominates for readability. Highlights use a tight line-height so the yellow sits close to the text.

<div className="not-prose mt-8">
  <div className="rounded-2xl border-2 border-zinc-200 bg-white p-12 dark:border-zinc-700 dark:bg-[#09090b]">
    <h2 className="text-4xl font-black leading-tight text-[#09090b] dark:text-white" style={{lineHeight: '1.15'}}>
      Driven Entrepreneurs{' '}
      <span className="bg-[#facc15] px-2 text-[#09090b]">Train Their Body</span>{' '}
      To Build A Business That Lasts.
    </h2>
    <div className="mt-6 text-lg leading-relaxed text-zinc-700 dark:text-zinc-300">
      Most business programs ignore your health. Most fitness programs ignore your business. UPA integrates both because you cannot sustain success in one without the other.
    </div>
    <div className="mt-8 flex flex-wrap gap-4">
      <button className="rounded-lg bg-[#09090b] px-6 py-3 font-semibold text-white dark:bg-white dark:text-[#09090b]">
        Learn More
      </button>
      <button className="rounded-lg bg-[#facc15] px-6 py-3 font-semibold text-[#09090b]">
        Get Started
      </button>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

### Best Practices for White Backgrounds

- **Black for body text**: Maximum readability (17.72:1 contrast)
- **Yellow for accents**: Highlights, badges, important CTAs
- **Never yellow body text**: Fails WCAG contrast requirements
- **Use yellow sparingly**: Strategic emphasis only

## All Caps Typography {{ id: 'all-caps-typography' }}

ALL CAPS with tight line-height creates powerful, commanding headlines. Keep the headline as a single paragraph with punctuation carrying the rhythm, not separate stacked lines.

<div className="not-prose mt-8">
  <div className="rounded-2xl bg-[#09090b] px-16 py-16 text-center">
    <h1 className="text-6xl font-black uppercase leading-[0.95] tracking-tight text-white">
      STOP WAITING. <span className="text-[#facc15]">START BUILDING.</span> BECOME UNSTOPPABLE.
    </h1>
  </div>
</div>

<Note>
  Use ALL CAPS sparingly. Great for hero sections and impactful statements. Never for body text or long-form content. Keep related statements in one paragraph with punctuation, not broken into separate stacked headings.
</Note>
